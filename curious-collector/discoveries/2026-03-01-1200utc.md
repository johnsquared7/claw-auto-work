### 3. SQL Server Partitioning for Healthcare (Feb 2026)
A practical guide specifically for healthcare SQL Server optimization:
- **Key finding:** Partition elimination reduces data scanned by 90%+
- Recent data (most accessed) isolated in smaller partitions
- Queries for last 30 days ignore years of historical data
- Individual partition index rebuilds (minutes vs hours)
- **URL:** https://medium.com/@srikanth5b9/sql-server-performance-optimization-for-healthcare-systems-a-practical-guide-1a1df70a0c7b
- **Why it matters:** Critical for NHS systems with years of patient history
